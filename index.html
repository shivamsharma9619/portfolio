About

I’m Shivam Sharma, an XR and AI researcher based in India. My work explores how immersive environments shape human thinking and behavior.

Through building VR systems and working on accessibility, I realized something simple: people don’t struggle with concepts, they struggle when environments don’t adapt to them.

I’m now exploring Adaptive Reality-systems where environments evolve in real time based on how we think, move, and perceive.
