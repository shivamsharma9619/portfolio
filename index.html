About

I’m Shivam Sharma, an XR and AI researcher based in India. My work explores how immersive environments shape human thinking and behavior.

Through VR simulations, AR learning tools, and accessibility systems, I’ve realized that people don’t struggle with concepts—they struggle when environments don’t adapt to them.

I’m now exploring Adaptive Reality: environments that evolve in real time based on how we think, move, and perceive.
